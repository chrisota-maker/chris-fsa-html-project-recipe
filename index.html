Gyoza Recipie

A Japanese style dumpling with Chinese origins.

Ingredients & Instructions

  • 300 g ground pork
  • 100 g Napa cabbage
  • 10 g scallion
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1/2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 10 ml soy sauce
  • 10 ml rice vinegar

  1. Slice cabage and place in bowl.
  2. Mix pork, cabbage, scallions, soy sauce, sake, mirin, and grated garlic in a bowl.
  3. Place gyoza wrapper in weaker hand.
  4. Fold the groza in half. Blah blah blah, words words words
  5. Heat your pan to a medium heat. Cook-it!
  6. Take off the lid, let cool, enjoy!

Tip: Make sure you have fun!
